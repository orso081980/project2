Marco Maffei

My name is Marco Maffei. I'm a developer, an awesome coder (maybe) and a responsive designer who is tring to improve his skills in pretty much all fields of this industry. I have different backgrounds because I built my knowledge in three different countries. I studied law at ”Università di Cagliari” in Italy. It allowed me to have my first approached with IT industry, because I found a job as a paralegal for an engineer company, where I worked for a few years. There, I developed a good attitude for web design since I worked closely to the web department of my company "Città Nuova" (my rule was to inspect legal document), until I realized that it was my dream job. Therefore I decided to deepen my English skill and I spent a year in Australia, where I improved and achieve a good language attitude in two of the best english school in Australia: SELC in Sydney (middle) and at English Unlimited in Brisbane (first). After a year, I was finally able, after I passed the IELTS exam, to enrol in the web designer and development course at Georgian College in Barrie,ON where I’m doing my forth semester, before doing my last semester this summer.
